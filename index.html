Safety Management Program Details

Course Overview

This program provides comprehensive training in occupational health and safety management, preparing students to identify, evaluate, and control workplace hazards.

Curriculum
  • Principles of Occupational Health and Safety
  • Risk Assessment and Management
  • Legal Requirements and Compliance
  • Incident Investigation and Reporting
  • Safety Management Systems
Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue careers as Safety Officers, Health and Safety Coordinators, or Safety Managers in various industries.

Requirements

Grade 12 certificate or equivalent. No prior experience required.

Computer Literacy

Essential computer skills for the modern workplace.

Computer Literacy Course Details

Course Description

This course provides foundational computer skills including operating systems, word processing, spreadsheets, presentations, internet usage, and basic troubleshooting.

Modules
  • Introduction to Computers
  • Microsoft Word Essentials
  • Microsoft Excel Basics
  • PowerPoint Presentations
  • Internet and Email
  • Basic Computer Maintenance
Duration

4 weeks (2 evening classes per week)

Certification

Participants receive a Certificate of Completion.
